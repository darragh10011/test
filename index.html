What is a Curated Memoir?

An interview-led narrative shaped through careful listening, ethical gathering, and respectful editing. We translate lived experience into readable, chaptered memoirs that preserve each person’s voice.

We partner with storytellers to craft a record that feels true to them.

About Curated Memoirs

Curated Memoirs by StoryKeepeer is a human-first process: we interview, listen, and shape narratives with care. Each memoir is built from consented conversations and edited into readable chapters that honor the storyteller’s intent.

Interview-led and ethically gathered

We begin with deep listening and clear boundaries. Storytellers decide what to share, how it’s used, and where it lives.

Voice-preserving, chapter-ready

We craft narratives that reflect the cadence, warmth, and truth of the original voice—then refine into a book-ready structure.

Respect, consent, and collaboration

Our team works closely with editors and storytellers to review drafts, handle sensitive details, and protect privacy.

The Role

Apply as an Interviewer, an Author/Writer, or both. Each role plays a distinct part in shaping a final memoir.

Interviewer

  • Prepare prompts tailored to each storyteller
  • Conduct audio or video interviews with care
  • Ask follow-up questions that uncover emotional truth
  • Organize notes and highlight key moments

Author / Writer

  • Craft narrative chapters from interviews or transcripts
  • Maintain voice and pacing while shaping a clear arc
  • Flag light fact-checking items for review
  • Collaborate with editors for refinement

Hybrid: Interviewer + Author

  • Lead interviews and convert them into chapters
  • Bridge emotional nuance from conversation to prose
  • Reduce handoffs and strengthen continuity
  • Support both fieldwork and editorial flow

What We Look For

We are seeking people who bring skill, care, and humility to personal storytelling.

Core capabilities

  • Empathy and respectful interviewing
  • Excellent listening and note organization
  • Ability to structure narratives clearly
  • Commitment to confidentiality and consent
  • Editing rigor and attention to detail
  • Reliability with project-based deadlines
  • Curiosity, patience, and cultural sensitivity

Nice to have

  • Multilingual interviewing or writing
  • Journalism, oral history, or documentary background
  • Developmental editing experience
  • Trauma-informed or sensitivity training

FAQ

Answers to common questions about working with Curated Memoirs by StoryKeepeer.

How often do projects become available?

Project availability varies. We work on a rolling basis and match opportunities to availability, experience, and subject matter fit.

How is compensation handled?

Compensation is discussed during the review stage and aligned to project scope, role, and complexity. We prioritize clarity and transparency before any work begins.

What tools do you use?

We typically use secure video or audio platforms, shared outlines, and collaborative editing tools. Tool details are provided during onboarding.

Is the work remote or asynchronous?

Yes. Interviews are scheduled with storytellers, and writing/editing can be completed remotely and asynchronously.

How do you handle consent and privacy?

Consent is central. Storytellers review what is captured, and we follow clear privacy guidelines. Sensitive details are handled with care and explicit approval.

What is your approach to AI?

Our work is human-led. We do not use AI to replace interviews or author voice. Any tooling must protect privacy and is reviewed in advance.

Can I apply for both roles?

Absolutely. If you have interviewing and writing experience, select “Both” and share samples that reflect each capability.
